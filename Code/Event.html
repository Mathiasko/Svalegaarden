Private events

Svalegaarden Most creates the best environment for every event. We want to help to turn your events into memorable experiences with focus on gastronomy and unique surroundings. Whether you are having a wedding party or a funeral, Svalegaarden Most is the best choice. We are always open to dialogue about your wishes, and we strive, to achieve the very best. We arrange parties for groups of at least 20 people.
